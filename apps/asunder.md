# ASUNDER

 Asunder is a graphical Audio CD ripper and encoder
 for Linux. You can use it to save tracks from an 
 Audio CD as any of WAV, MP3, OGG, FLAC, Opus, 
 WavPack, Musepack, AAC, and Monkey's Audio files.
